About Us

Welcome to JAICO Fabrication – Building Tomorrow, Today.

Our Story

Founded in 2014 by Mr. and Mrs. Alexander Muiruri, JAICO Fabrication started as a hardware store in Utawala. Named after their children, JAICO is a family-driven business that has since transformed into a specialized fabrication company.

Who We Serve

We work with builders, contractors, and homeowners who seek quality fabrication services. Our clients include families building homes, contractors managing large projects such as Kigumo Level 5 Hospital, Ulinzi Boys High School, and more.

What We Do

We specialize in powder-coated metalwork—a durable finish that ensures long-lasting quality and protection. From restaurant furniture to structural components, our expertise meets the highest standards of durability and craftsmanship.

Our Vision

To be the region’s most recognized brand in general construction services and materials.

Our Mission

Buildings that stand the test of time, so as to serve future generations.

Our Values

  • 1. Duty to God
  • 2. Duty to Land
  • 3. Duty to Family
